About Us

Our Story

Yaka Brand Fashion is not just clothing—it’s a rebellion against forgotten history. Born from the shadows of Sri Lanka’s 5,000-year-old Yaksha legends, we fuse ancient demonic energy with modern streetwear.

Our designs channel the raw power of Demon King Ravana, Mahasona, Gara Yaka, and other ancestral spirits, transforming myth into wearable art. We are the voice of the unseen, the storytellers of the occult, and the bridge between Sri Lanka’s hidden past and the future of fashion.

OUR MISSION

To resurrect Sri Lanka’s darkest legends through bold, symbolic fashion giving a new generation the power to wear their heritage with pride.

  • Revive the untold stories of Yaksha mythology.
  • Empower wearers with designs that embody strength, mystery, and rebellion.
  • Challenge mainstream fashion with limited-edition, myth-infused streetwear.

OUR VISION

To build the Yaka Empire—a global movement where:

  • Every T-shirt is a sacred artifact—carrying the energy of ancient demons.
  • Sri Lanka’s occult history is recognized worldwide.
  • Streetwear becomes a ritual—where fashion meets folklore.
